In 2017, I  completed an internship at the [German Historical Institute in Paris](https://www.dhi-paris.fr/), where I annotated 1,162 Falkenstein regests of medieval charters according to a custom annotation scheme. These data were subsequently processed further and have since been published. 

[See, for example, the Reims Archibischops.](https://qed.perspectivia.net/gallia-pontificia-online/regesten/reims1/regest/1)
