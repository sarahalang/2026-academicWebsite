In my role as Head of Digital Humanities at the Max Planck Institute for the History of Science, 
I am currently editing a volume tentatively titled *Digital History of Science and Knowledge*. 

It brings together contributions that explore both methodological innovations and the particular challenges and opportunities that arise when computational approaches are used in these fields,
showcasing work from the Max Planck Institute for the History of Science.
