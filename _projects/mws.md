[The _Spectators_ project, also known as _Moralische Wochenschriften_,](https://gams.uni-graz.at/mws) was the first digital humanities project in which I was employed as a student assistant. My task was to correct XML data and XSLT transformations within the portal. As this was a long-running project, the work primarily involved maintenance and further development of existing infrastructure.
